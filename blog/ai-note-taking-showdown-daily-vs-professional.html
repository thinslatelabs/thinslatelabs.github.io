How You Capture: Multi-Modal vs Text-First

Your daily capture habits reveal which app suits you better.

OktoNote: True Multi-Modal Capture

OktoNote embraces how you naturally capture information throughout your day:

  • Voice recordings: Perfect for capturing thoughts while commuting, exercising, or when your hands are busy
  • Photo capture: Snap pictures of documents, whiteboards, food labels, or anything visual that matters
  • Document parsing: Upload PDFs, receipts, or forms and let AI extract key information

This multi-modal approach means you can capture information however it naturally occurs in your life, without forcing everything into text format.
