OktoNote

An AI-powered note-taking app that transforms any input - text, voice, photos, or documents - into organized knowledge cards. OktoNote automatically categorizes your content as tasks, todos, journals, itineraries, links, attachments, lists, discussion notes, details to remember, and study materials. Features include offline-first functionality, intelligent semantic search, and a beautiful paper-inspired interface.
