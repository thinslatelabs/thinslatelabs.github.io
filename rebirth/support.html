Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the widget to my home screen?
  1. Long-press on your iPhone home screen until apps start wiggling
  2. Tap the "+" button in the top-left corner
  3. Search for "Rebirth" or scroll to find it
  4. Select the widget size (Large recommended)
  5. Tap "Add Widget"
  6. Position the widget where you'd like it
  7. Tap "Done" in the top-right corner
How do I change my affirmation categories?
  1. Open the Rebirth app
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear icon) in the top-right
  3. Tap "Affirmation Preferences"
  4. Select or deselect categories (minimum 5 required)
  5. Tap "Save" to apply your changes

Your new categories will be reflected in both the app and widget.

My widget isn't updating after purchase - what should I do?

After purchasing the lifetime access, your widget should update automatically within 5 minutes. However, for an instant update:

  1. Look for the refresh button (circular arrow icon) on your widget
  2. Tap the refresh button to immediately check your purchase status
  3. The widget will switch from the marketing view to showing affirmations

If you still have issues, try restarting your device or contact support.

How does the lifetime purchase work?

Rebirth offers a one-time lifetime purchase that includes:

  • Unlimited access to all 22 affirmation categories
  • Beautiful home screen widgets
  • All current and future features
  • No recurring fees or subscriptions

Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I change fonts and backgrounds?
  1. Open the Rebirth app
  2. Tap the Settings icon
  3. Tap "Font" to choose from 4 beautiful font options
  4. Tap "Background" to switch between:
    • Category-Based: Unique images for each category
    • Standard: Gradient background

Your choices apply to both the app and widget.

Where do the quotes and affirmations come from?

Our affirmations come from carefully curated sources including:

  • Inspirational quotes and wisdom literature
  • Motivational and self-help content
  • Original affirmations created by our team

All content is reviewed to ensure it's uplifting, positive, and appropriate for daily inspiration.

How often does the widget update with new affirmations?

The widget automatically refreshes every 2 hours with a new affirmation. You can also:

  • Tap the shuffle button (in the app or on the widget) for an instant new affirmation
  • Open the app to see a new affirmation

Time-of-day filtering ensures you get morning motivation, afternoon boosts, or evening reflections automatically.

Is Rebirth available for Android?

Rebirth is currently available exclusively for iOS (iPhone and iPad). We're focusing on delivering the best possible experience for iOS users, including beautiful widgets and seamless integration with iOS features.

Follow us on Instagram @rebirth.app for updates on potential Android support in the future.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
  1. Install Rebirth on your new device
  2. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase
  3. Your purchase will be automatically restored
  4. If needed, tap the refresh button on the widget to update its status
Do you collect my personal data?

No! Rebirth is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We don't collect any personal information
  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • All your preferences stay on your device
  • Only purchase data is processed by RevenueCat for validation
